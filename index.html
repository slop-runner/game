HOW TO PLAY

EXPERTISE SIMULATOR — READ BEFORE YOUR FIRST ROUND

1
CHOOSE A DOMAIN
Select one of five thinking frameworks — Design, Business, Market, User, or Project. Each domain tests a different type of expert intuition. Your mastery per domain is tracked separately.
2
READ THE SCENARIO
A realistic business or product brief drops in. It sounds plausible — but it contains exactly one embedded flaw. Your job is to find it. The 90-second timer starts immediately.
3
INJECT MENTAL CUES
The Injection Deck contains Mental Cue cards. Click cues that match what you're seeing. Once selected, a cue is locked — you cannot deselect it. On submission, green = correct, red = wrong selection. You can select multiple cues.
4
SUBMIT YOUR VERDICT
Write a short verdict in the text box — name the flaw and state which principle exposes it. Then hit SUBMIT VERDICT. Speed matters: the more time remaining, the higher your bonus.
5
SEE THE RESULT
The real flaw is revealed with a full explanation. You earn XP, build streaks, and track mastery per domain. Play again to face a different scenario — scenarios are AI-generated so the pool is infinite.
